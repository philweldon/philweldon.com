What is KDCR Search Lab?

KDCR Search Lab turns one of the trickiest skills in legal technology — searching large document collections — into a game you can learn by playing. Every round drops you into a unique, fictional legal case: a dispute, an investigation, a discovery request. Your job is to find the documents that matter.

The twist: you solve the same case four different ways, using the four search methods real eDiscovery professionals use every day. Then a scorecard shows you exactly what each method found, what it missed, and why — so you build real intuition for when each technique shines and when it fails.

The four search methods

K

Keyword

The classic. Search for exact words and phrases. Fast and precise — until people use synonyms, abbreviations, or code words.

D

dtSearch

Power-user syntax: boolean operators, wildcards, and proximity searches that find words near each other.

C

Concept

Search by meaning, not exact words. Finds documents about a topic even when they never use your search terms.

R

RAG

Retrieval-augmented generation — ask questions in plain English and let AI retrieve and reason over the documents.

How a round works

  1. Get your case. A fictional legal scenario is generated just for you — no two rounds are the same.
  2. Read the brief. Understand the dispute and your search objective.
  3. Browse the documents. Emails, memos, and records — some relevant, some red herrings.
  4. Search four ways. Run Keyword, dtSearch, Concept, and RAG searches against the same objective.
  5. Review your scorecard. See what each method caught and missed, with teaching notes explaining why.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

KDCR Search Lab is designed to collect as little as possible:

  • No accounts. The app has no sign-up, login, or user profiles.
  • No personal data collected. We do not collect, store, or sell your name, email, location, contacts, or device identifiers.
  • AI-generated content. Game scenarios and search queries you enter are sent to our server, which uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate cases and process gameplay. Queries are used only to power the game and are not linked to your identity.
  • No tracking. The app contains no advertising, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • TestFlight. During beta testing, Apple may share crash logs and basic usage statistics with the developer under Apple's own privacy terms.
