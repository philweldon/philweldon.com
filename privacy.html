KDCR SEARCH LAB

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated June 8, 2026

Overview

KDCR Search Lab is an educational game that teaches eDiscovery search methods. This policy explains how the app handles information. The short version: we collect nothing about you.

Information We Collect

KDCR Search Lab does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.

How the App Works

Each game round, the app sends a prompt to a secure server to generate a fictional legal scenario. This prompt contains no personal information about you — only instructions for generating game content. The server processes the request and returns fictional case content.

Third-Party Services

The app uses a private server to generate game content using AI. No personal data is transmitted as part of this process. The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

Because the app collects no personal information, there is nothing to share with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
