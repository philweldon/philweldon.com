About

Phil Weldon is a nationally recognized eDiscovery and legal AI leader, currently serving as Senior Director of eDiscovery and AI Assisted Review at Hecker Fink LLP. He founded and scaled the firm’s eDiscovery Technology Department, leads enterprise AI strategy and review innovation, and has been named to the Lawdragon 100 Leading Artificial Intelligence & Legal Tech Advisors list in both 2025 and 2026.

A frequent speaker at major legal technology forums including Legalweek, ILTA, Relativity Fest, Microsoft, Google, and NYSBA/Perrin, Phil focuses on making advanced AI, TAR, automation, and complex discovery workflows practical for litigators, clients, and legal operations teams.
