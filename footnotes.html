Search Objective
🔍 Keyword / Boolean Search
Exact word matching. Use AND, OR, NOT to combine terms. Only finds documents with your exact words.
Examples: missing AND attachment  •  deadline OR "filing date"
⚡ dtSearch — Advanced Syntax
Adds wildcards, proximity, fuzzy matching, and stemming on top of Boolean logic.
attach* wildcards  •  miss* w/5 attach* proximity  •  attachement~ fuzzy
🧠 Conceptual / Vector Search
Search by meaning, not exact words. Finds documents with similar concepts even when different vocabulary is used.
Example: someone knew the file was not included before the deadline
🤖 RAG — AI Answer Generation
Ask a question. The AI retrieves relevant documents and generates an answer with citations. Quality depends entirely on what it retrieves.
Example: What happened with the missing attachment?
Try all 4 modes for the best learning experience.
